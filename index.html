Frequently Asked Questions

+ What tools were used in eMarc?

eMarc uses React for the frontend and Google Sheets for data storage, integrated via Google Apps Script for automation.

+ How do I deploy the application?

Follow the steps in the How to Deploy section, including configuring Google Sheets and Scripts access and updating the config JSON.

+ How is the application maintained?

Regularly update Google Sheets and verify access permissions for uninterrupted functionality.

+ Where can I find examples of sheets and scripts?

Examples are available in the project's samples folder, along with Google Sheets templates and Google Scripts.
